6gen3 Site Redesign Case Study

Who:

6gen3

What:

Website Redesign

When:

A week

Where:

Desktop and Mobile Phone Browser

How:

Paper and Pencil, Figma

Steps

User Research

Company directors wanted a change in the look and focus of the company website.

Ideation

I viewed the existing site and saw where the deficiencies lay, then sketched up something that was cleaner, more visually appealing, and more functional.
The new site needed to better represent the companies' products.

Sketching

Created the initial sketch

Prototyping

Created the mockup in Figma

Retrospective

There may be some differences between the final product and the Figma mockups. Since I am the sole person making decisions, it seemed faster to incorporate them directly into the final product, vs. taking the time to modify the mockup.
Once the site starts getting reviewed, I will incorporate those suggestions into the next version iteration.
